(03) — Our Story

The Mount's favourite
cafe, bar & venue.

Somethin' Somethin' is The Mount's favourite licensed cafe, bar, and event venue. We're the crew behind the viral matcha drinks, the sell-out offcuts, and the newly covered sunniest deck in the Bay of Plenty.

Breakfast, brunch, Friday night dinners, weekend bottomless brunch, private functions, and out-catering… we do it all, and we do it with heart.

Come find us at 2D Heath Street, Mount Maunganui. We'd love to have you!
